JRM - Arts and Games

The mission of the website is to showcase the hobbies, interest, and talent of us - the web designers. The focus of this website is mostly to show our artworks and the games we play. We decided to pick something quite unique yet inspiring which is to focus on combining two concepts or art which is gaming and traditional art with slight interest of animation.
